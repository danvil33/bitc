Information we collect

BitC Network may collect account details, email address, device information, app usage information, support messages, wallet-related activity, and technical data needed to operate and improve the platform.

How we use information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers such as hosting, analytics, authentication, security, and app distribution providers where needed to operate BitC Network.

We aim to apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No digital platform can guarantee 100% security, so users should protect their login credentials, avoid suspicious links, and use official BitC channels only.

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may request access, correction, deletion, or limitation of certain personal information.
