Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BitC Network

How does mobile mining work?

BitC Network uses a unique consensus algorithm that allows your phone to participate in network validation without the intensive computational work of traditional mining. This makes it energy-efficient and accessible to everyone.

Is BitC Network safe for my device?

Absolutely! Our app is designed with device safety as a top priority. It uses minimal battery, doesn't overheat your phone, and respects your privacy with secure encryption protocols.

When can I withdraw my earnings?

You can withdraw your BITC tokens once you reach the minimum withdrawal threshold. This helps keep transaction fees low for all users. The current threshold is displayed in the app.

What makes BitC different from other mobile miners?

BitC Network focuses on sustainability, security, and user experience. Our energy-efficient algorithm, robust security measures, and intuitive interface set us apart from other mobile mining solutions.
