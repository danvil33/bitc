Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn with Bitc mining? +
Earnings vary based on network participation and market conditions. On average, active users earn between 5-15 Bitc tokens daily.
Does mining affect my phone's battery life? +
Our optimized algorithm minimizes battery usage. Most users report less than 5% additional battery drain during active mining sessions.
Is there a fee to withdraw my mined Bitc? +
A small network fee applies to withdrawals, typically around 1% of the transaction amount. There are no fees for mining or storing your Bitc.
Can I mine on multiple devices? +
Yes, you can use up to 3 devices with the same account. However, mining rewards are optimized per account, not per device.
