Introduction

The Blockchain and Internet Technology Coin (BITC) is a revolutionary decentralized digital currency focused on sustainable, user-friendly mining through innovative free energy technology. BITC aims to bridge blockchain utility with internet-scale accessibility while maintaining environmental consciousness and user empowerment.

Core Innovation

BITC introduces a groundbreaking approach to cryptocurrency mining by eliminating traditional barriers of hardware costs and excessive energy consumption. Our free-energy mining protocol enables anyone with a smartphone or computer to participate in network validation and earn rewards, democratizing access to cryptocurrency mining.

Traditional cryptocurrencies face significant challenges including massive energy consumption, centralization of mining power, and accessibility barriers for average users. BITC systematically addresses these issues through its innovative consensus mechanism, equitable distribution model, and mobile-first approach that prioritizes user experience and environmental sustainability.

Advanced Burn Mechanism for Price Support

BITC implements an aggressive, multi-tiered burn mechanism that systematically removes tokens from circulation as network usage and adoption increases:

  • Transaction Burns: 0.05% of every transaction permanently burned from supply
  • Velocity Burns: Additional burns triggered during high transaction velocity periods
  • Milestone Burns: Strategic burns at key adoption and development milestones
  • Revenue Burns: 50% of all network fees allocated to buyback and burn programs

This sophisticated deflationary pressure combined with growing utility creates strong, sustainable upward price momentum, ensuring BITC maintains values well above $1.00 throughout all growth phases and market conditions.
