Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn with BitC mining? +
Earnings vary based on network participation, market conditions, and your level of activity. On average, active users earn between 5-15 BITC tokens daily through regular mining. Additional rewards are available through our referral program, which allows you to earn a percentage of the tokens mined by users you refer to the platform.
Does mining affect my phone's battery life? +
Our optimized algorithm is specifically designed to minimize battery impact. Most users report less than 5% additional battery drain during active mining sessions. The application automatically adjusts its resource usage based on your device's current battery level and can be configured to pause mining activities when battery levels drop below a specified threshold.
Is there a fee to withdraw my mined BitC? +
A small network fee applies to withdrawals, typically around 1% of the transaction amount. This fee helps maintain the blockchain infrastructure and ensures fast, secure transactions. There are no fees for mining activities or storing your BITC tokens within the application wallet. We periodically offer fee-free withdrawal promotions to our most active users.
Can I mine on multiple devices? +
Yes, you can use up to 3 devices with the same account. However, mining rewards are optimized per account rather than per device to ensure fair distribution across the network. This approach prevents abuse of the system while allowing users the flexibility to mine from different devices according to their convenience and daily routine.
