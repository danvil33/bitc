Introduction

The Blockchain and Internet Technology Coin (BITC) is a decentralized digital currency focused on sustainable, user-friendly mining through free energy technology. BITC aims to connect blockchain utility with internet-scale accessibility.

Core Innovation

BITC introduces a revolutionary approach to cryptocurrency mining by eliminating the traditional barriers of hardware costs and excessive energy consumption. Our free-energy mining protocol allows anyone with a smartphone or computer to participate in network validation and earn rewards.

Traditional cryptocurrencies face significant challenges related to energy consumption, centralization of mining power, and accessibility barriers for average users. BITC addresses these issues through its innovative consensus mechanism and equitable distribution model.

Burn Mechanism for Price Support

BITC implements an aggressive burn mechanism that removes tokens from circulation as network usage increases:

  • Transaction Burns: 0.05% of every transaction permanently burned
  • Velocity Burns: Additional burns triggered during high transaction velocity periods
  • Milestone Burns: Strategic burns at key adoption milestones
  • Revenue Burns: 50% of network fees used for buyback and burn

This deflationary pressure combined with growing utility creates strong upward price momentum, ensuring BITC maintains values well above $1.00 throughout its growth phases.
