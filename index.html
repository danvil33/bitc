How much can I earn with Bitc mining? +
Earnings vary based on network participation and market conditions. On average, active users earn between 5-15 Bitc tokens daily, with potential for more through referrals.
Does mining affect my phone's battery life? +
Our optimized algorithm minimizes battery usage. Most users report less than 5% additional battery drain during active mining sessions, making it phone-friendly.
Is there a fee to withdraw my mined Bitc? +
A small network fee applies to withdrawals, typically around 1% of the transaction amount. There are no fees for mining or storing your Bitc in the app.
